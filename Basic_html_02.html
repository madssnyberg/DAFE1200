One more basic thing..

With the 'paragraph', extra spaces and line-shifts are ignored.

If u need to retain such formating, for example lyrics, the easiest way is to use <pre>

    Du må ikke sitte
    trygt i dieie
    mande iomd e fs
    daw iie mmdoo
    dawpdaw fes.

<pre> and <blockquote> do similar thing, but as you can see with 'blockquote' you loose extra formating, and a citation is expected.

Du må ikke sitte trygt i dieie mande iomd e fs daw iie mmdoo dawpdaw fes.
